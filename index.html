After your order has been shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to check the status of your delivery on the carrier's website.
The delivery time depends on the shipping method you choose and your location. Typically, standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, and expedited shipping takes 2-3 business days. You can check the estimated delivery time at checkout.
You can return items within 30 days of purchase. Items must be in their original condition and packaging. If you need to initiate a return, please contact our support team.
Shipping costs are calculated based on the destination, shipping method, and the total weight of your order. You will be able to view the exact shipping cost during the checkout process.
If your order has not yet been processed, you may be able to modify or cancel it. Please contact our support team immediately for assistance.
